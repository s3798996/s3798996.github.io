Job Description

A description (in your own words) of the position, and particularly what makes this position appeals to you?

The appealing in this particular position is quite interesting. First of all, this position is not only developing and designing apps but also collaborating with people from different fields for ideas that could benefit the enterprise. Nonetheless, I have a major interest in creating user-interface as well as maintaining the application in order to bring the best user experience while using the app.

A description (in your own words) of the skills, qualifications, and experience required for the position.

- The requirement for a react native developer in this company is considered to be mediocre since it is not too demanding or less demanding. The main aspects of this position are to have both experiences from mobile development and react native. However, for the mobile development asset, they only required 1.5 years of experiences whereas react native in which the company need approximately 1 year of experience

- Nonetheless, it is mandatory to have a wide range of knowledge about building tools such as Xcode, AndroidStudio and Grandle . Plus, cloud-based messaging is also a crucial part when developing an app. On the other hand, react native app integration and balanced APIs are also needed for this position

- Besides programming criteria, the company needs several ranges of soft skills such as communication, teamwork, process, and self-motivation.

A description (in your own words) of the skills, qualifications, and experience you currently have?

- Despite having a little information about coding and security, communication and teamwork are the two top priorities in which I currently have at the moment. Both of these soft skills are necessary in every workplace or group project since it could bring satisfactory from both consumers and employees. Moreover, I also have minor experience of being a leader

A plan describing how you will obtain the skills, qualifications, and experience required for the position, building on those you have now. This need not be greatly detailed, (and will probably change significantly over time anyway), but try to be as specific as you can?

- First of all , the one thing that I have learned before entering university was that having an abundance of experiences is more critical than a high GPA . Although it doesn't mean that grade means nothing, it only provides a number on how well you go on a project or a test which is given by a professor.

- Furthermore, it is necessary to understand that having a great GPA score is only one tick out of two criteria in every job prospective.

- The course provides a long-term project which is about initiating a project with 3-4 people. Within this assignment, I would train my skills in putting my idea to real life and as well as sharpening both of my communication and teamwork skills.

- In order to get familiar with mobile development, I will apply for an internship on the 2nd semester since I could get familiar with professional and as well as, learning the concept
