Project Idea

SGPhone

Overview

- This project will be aiming for the concept of my own smartphone. Within this idea will be covered about the motivation behind this prospective technology along with a further description of the manufacturing process and several highlighted features. Furthermore, this particular phone will be the first phone to be used by a recycle materials ranging from used phones to the tiny chips on the old motherboard system .Nonetheless, this concept is not about establishing a successful smartphone and deliver to the market nor inspiring others young enthusiasts from the tech industries. But, it is about sending a clear message to the world that electronic waste should not have existed within the waste chart system and due to consumer demands, it has turned into a part that equivalent to food waste.

Motivation

- The story behind this particular idea was a dream that i used to have back in my high school years. I was interested in buying a new phone since i got my Iphone 4s stolen from a charity race. Furthermore, my mom offered me to have a sizable budget for a new phone, so within that moment, I initiate my research. My research includes not only the price range, but it also includes stability, materials and system requirements. After getting a hand on a new phone( Moto X Style) , I began following MKBHD for his smartphone review and Android Authorities for their overall information about display types, chipset and OS. Fast forward a couple of years later, I started to notice a pattern on the smartphone industry that every new smartphone always released with no other changes beside faster CPU, more ram, more storage, and better display. However, on the OS side, there are only IOS and Android in which a huge dominance on the smartphone OS market but there isn't any new OS declare on the market. Nonetheless, I witness a lot of people replacing their phones too frequently and even though it is around 1-2 years old. From that, i started spectating the consumer behaviour and the consequences in which that could damage future generations.

Description

- SGphone will become the phone that set the main focus on environment, stability, and usability. The structure of this particular device is straightforward, the exterior casing will be made out of recycled aluminum, thus the material consists of variety back case phone in which being collected from used phones. In addition, this phone will be equipped with an LCD display along with Fluid Color Tone, meaning the display will detect light and temperature on the surrounding environment and slowly configuring the RGB colours in order to save battery and avoid reflection from light with higher nits. Environmentally, this LCD will also be made out of used LCD screens taken from the recycle phone parts. Furthermore, the chipset and ram will also be made with recycled materials, although the process of transferring recycle materials into these 2 critical parts would be complicated since it might involve several negotiations from the Qualcomm manufacturer. Moreover, the phone will come with SnapDragon 855 along with 6GB of memory and in terms of memories, there will be some heavy tweaks with the multi-tasking function since it would create a smooth experience toward the users. The phone will be equipped with 5.5 LCD with 2K resolution display and within the display, there is one unique layer called AML (Anti Marks Layers) and the function of this particular layout is to prevent fingerprint mark from the display, meaning that whenever the user swipe or press, it will automatically leave a dirty smudge mark on the screen . But with the implementation of this layer, phone users will no longer concerned about having a dirty display. Besides the phone’s display, SGphone’s battery capacity can hold approximately 3200mAh in which can withstand at least a day with a single charge. However, the battery cannot be recycled due to numerous reasons but one of the most noticeable explanation is that the battery contains heavy metals which could harm both environment and people’s health or specifically, it contains several doses of toxic chemicals that should be contained. Moving onto the camera system, the phone will come with an optimized primary camera that able to shoot telephoto and changing its ISO number. Nonetheless, it is also equipped with multi lenses in order to achieve the highest colour accuracy in a picture. The secondary camera will get its minimal tweaks and lenses from the main camera. The porting and button systems on this device consist of USB-C, DnD and wake switches and a 3.5mm headphone jack. Since every smartphone brands are going wireless, I believe the headphone jack is still relevant for the majority of users. In terms of software, this particular phone will not be operated by Android nor IOS, but rather our own structured operating system called FluidOS.

FluidOS will bring a simplistic experience toward the consumers with a graphical UI, ideal navigation, smooth performance several tasks and utilization between the main hardware. Within this software, there is a key feature that can enable the storage unit to identify an unnecessary cache from apps and by the time the redundant cache is detected, it will automatically be deleted.

Tools and Technologies

- The software will be programmed by C language for the operating system and JavaScript for GUI since it has been used by multiple developers from Microsoft and Apple.

- As for the hardware, firstly, the phone case will be created by using the smelting technique since the parts were from old recycled phones. Secondly, the camera, chips and ram are the challenging aspects, it is because that Qualcomm would not allow an ordinary consumer to access their market unless there is some logical reasoning that would change their mind. Thirdly, the LCD screen would require an extension in order to others used screen together

      Outcome

      In case of an unexpected success from this particular project, it means that we have achieved our main objective which is delivering a message about the electronic waste that can be recycled into new functional smartphones. However, the outcome of this project would lead to consideration from the consumer market. Judging from this project, it could potentially have a side effect which is an inspiration for the young enthusiast who would want to construct a device using parts from the waste. On the other hand, the phone might be praised for using our own operating system since there are several smartphone brands always using none other operating systems besides Android.
